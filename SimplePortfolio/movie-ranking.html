My Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time:

The Aristocats (1970)

The story is simple but engaging with a positive message. The animation is beautiful and expressive and the music is catchy and memorable. The characters are all likable and relatable, their interactions are often funny and heartwarming.

Anchors Aweigh (1945)

It's a feel good movie with a great cast - Gene Kelly, Frank Sinatra, and Kathryn Grayson. The production values are top-notch. The film was shot in Technicolor, and the visuals are stunning with excellent music and dancing.

Hello Dolly! (1969)

The score by Jerry Herman is one of the best in musical theater history - the songs are catchy, well-written, and perfectly suited to the characters and story. The performances are top-notch with Barbra Streisand in her element as Dolly Levi. The direction by Gene Kelly is energetic and stylish.
